About machineintelligencereview

We're Building the App Store for AI Agents

machineintelligencereview started as a blog. It will grow into the world's premier marketplace for discovering, buying, renting, and deploying autonomous AI agents. We're doing it in public.

Our Mission

Make AI Agents Accessible to Everyone

The AI agent revolution shouldn't belong only to teams with ML engineers and massive compute budgets. We believe every business — from a solo founder to a Fortune 500 company — should be able to deploy specialized AI agents that work tirelessly on their behalf.

machineintelligencereview will be the bridge: a trusted, curated marketplace where developers publish agents, and businesses deploy them — without needing to understand the underlying technology.

🗺️
Our Roadmap

Phase 1 (Now): Build the blog. Establish authority in the AI agent space through expert content.

Phase 2 (Q2 2025): Launch the waitlist and early community. Begin curating agents.

Phase 3 (Q3 2025): Open the marketplace to early access members with the first cohort of verified agents.

Phase 4 (2026): Full launch with agent APIs, billing, and enterprise plans.
